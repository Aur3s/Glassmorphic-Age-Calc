# ⏳ Life Journey | Smart Age Calculator

A cinematic, premium-tier Age Calculator built with modern web technologies. Designed with a sleek mobile-first "Glassmorphism" interface, this tool provides pinpoint accurate tracking of a user's life timeline down to the exact year, month, and day.

## ✨ Features

* **Glassmorphic Phone Shell Design:** A stunning UI mimicking a modern smartphone interface with vibrant, premium gradient backdrops and blur effects.
* **Pinpoint Chronological Accuracy:** Flawlessly accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and prevents future date selections.
* **Dynamic Progress Ring:** Visually displays the exact percentage of the current year that has passed.
* **Deep Timeline Breakdown:** Computes total lifetime weeks, total lifetime days, the specific day of the week you were born, and your astrological Zodiac sign.
* **Next Birthday Countdown:** Real-time day-tracking telling you exactly how long until your next milestone.
* **Smart Native Alerts:** Integrated custom notification toasts for errors and success states.
* **Birthday Confetti Surprise:** Triggers a dual-cannon canvas confetti celebration if the calculated date matches the user's birthday!
* **Instant Clipboard Sharing:** One-click sharing button that copies a stylized summary of your results to your clipboard.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Ecosystem

* **HTML5 & CSS3** (Custom UI Architecture & Keyframe Animations)
* **Vanilla JavaScript** (ES6+ Logic & Date Objects)
* **FontAwesome v6.4.0** (Sleek vector iconography)
* **Canvas Confetti v1.6.0** (High-performance script-driven animations)
* **Google Fonts** (Cairo & Tajawal typography)
